Let's Collaborate

Aloha offers an ideal space to hold retreats, workshops and other events. Boasting a beautiful, large temple, comfortable accommodations, delicious vegan food, hot showers and more, we invite you to facilitate your event at our home in the stunning Sierra Nevada.

Tailored Packages

It's up to you how much you would like us to be involved. Whether you'd prefer we simply take care of the facilities and catering or be more involved in facilitating community-style activities, we're happy to tailor-make a package to suit your needs. Optional activities provided by us include:

Yoga and meditation classes

Evening fires with live music

Cacao ceremonies

Day hikes to waterfalls and/or Minca (local town)

Overnight hike to Cerro Kennedy, featuring views of sweeping valleys and snow peaks

Nutrition workshops and fermentation classes

Permaculture theory and techniques

Cleansing mud baths and herb baths

Please contact us for more information or to express interest.
