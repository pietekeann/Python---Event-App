Our Farm

We, at Aloha, live by the principles of permaculture, with the aim to cultivate intensive and diverse agricultural systems and to build with sustainable methods and materials local to our region. With an ever-expanding garden, our ultimate goal is to be self-sustainable, growing as much food on our own land as we can in order to meet our needs. In the meantime, we source available fruits and vegetables from our farming neighbours to support our local economy and local food production.

Through the philosophy of permaculture, we are able to feel the empowerment and guidance to work closely with the land, and to form a deep and harmonious connection with our surroundings. We believe that by working towards spiritual evolution within ourselves, we can manifest abundance in our co-created material realm.
