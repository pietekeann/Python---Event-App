How To Get Here

From Santa Marta, the cheapest option is to take a colectivo (shared mini-bus) from the Minca station in the central market (8.000 COP). Alternatively, you can take a taxi or moto. Motos leave Santa Marta from Buenavista, Mamatoco and Yucal. When you arrive in Minca, you have 3 options to get to us: you can walk for free (2-4 hours, depending on physical abilities), take a mototaxi (40mins, 25.000 COP) or hire a car (50mins, prices available at the CootrasMinca Station).
